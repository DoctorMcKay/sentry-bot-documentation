        Sentry is provided with absolutely no warranty or guarantee of future service. While we will make every effort to keep Sentry running with uptime as close to 100% as possible, systems do fail and Sentry may become unavailable from time to time. Additionally, defects and changes in Discord's systems may cause features to stop working properly.

        # Abuse

        We reserve the right to terminate our relationship with you at any time for actions which we deem as abusive. Abusive actions include, but are not limited to denial of service attacks, flooding the bot with bogus commands, or otherwise exploiting a defect or vulnerability in Sentry's programming.

        Your privacy and the privacy of your server members are very important to us. Check out the privacy policy for a full listing of data we collect and how we protect it.
